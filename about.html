Our Story

Born from a
Passion for Connection

Café De La Parix began as a dream shared over a single cup of coffee — the belief that a truly great café could do more than serve drinks. It could build community, ignite conversations, and become the heartbeat of a neighbourhood.

From humble beginnings, we've grown into Gaborone's most beloved gathering place, where locals and visitors come not just for the food, but for the feeling of belonging.

Our mission is woven into every decision we make — from sourcing our beans to composting our grounds. We believe cafés have a responsibility to their community and their planet.
